🔥 Today's Workout

Duration: 52 minutes

Difficulty: ★★★★★

🏃 Warmup (5 minutes)

  • Jumping jacks - 30 seconds
  • Arm circles - 20 forward, 20 backward
  • Leg swings - 10 each direction
  • Bodyweight squats - 10 reps
  • High knees - 20 seconds

💪 Main Exercises

1. Turkish Get-up

Sets: 6 | Reps: 14 | Rest: 90s

Target: core

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Slow controlled movement, keep weight stable overhead, practice without weight first

Make it harder: Add pause at bottom, Increase tempo, Add resistance

2. Dumbbell Chest Press

Sets: 6 | Reps: 14 | Rest: 90s

Target: chest

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Control the weight, full range of motion, don't arch back excessively

Make it harder: Add pause at bottom, Increase tempo, Add resistance

3. Dumbbell Deadlift

Sets: 6 | Reps: 14 | Rest: 90s

Target: hamstrings

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Chest up, shoulders back, weight in heels, neutral spine

Make it harder: Add pause at bottom, Increase tempo, Add resistance

4. Kettlebell Swing

Sets: 6 | Reps: 14 | Rest: 90s

Target: glutes

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Hip hinge movement, arms relaxed, power from hips

Make it harder: Add pause at bottom, Increase tempo, Add resistance

  • Deep breathing - 1 minute
  • Forward fold stretch - 30 seconds
