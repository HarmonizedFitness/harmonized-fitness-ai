🔥 Today's Workout

Duration: 52 minutes

Difficulty: ★★★☆☆

Progression: Building upon previous sessions with increased challenge

🏃 Warmup (5 minutes)

  • Jumping jacks - 30 seconds
  • Arm circles - 20 forward, 20 backward
  • Leg swings - 10 each direction
  • Bodyweight squats - 10 reps
  • High knees - 20 seconds

💪 Main Exercises

1. Dumbbell Chest Press

Sets: 2 | Reps: 8 | Rest: 60s

Target: chest

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Control the weight, full range of motion, don't arch back excessively

2. Dumbbell Deadlift

Sets: 2 | Reps: 8 | Rest: 60s

Target: hamstrings

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Chest up, shoulders back, weight in heels, neutral spine

3. Pulse Squats

Sets: 2 | Reps: 8 | Rest: 60s

Target: quadriceps

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Stay in squat position throughout pulses, maintain tension, breathe through the burn, full extension between reps

4. Push-up

Sets: 2 | Reps: 8 | Rest: 60s

Target: chest

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Keep core tight, straight line from head to heels, full range of motion

5. Plank

Sets: 2 | Reps: 8 | Rest: 60s

Target: core

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Engage core, neutral spine, breathe normally, no sagging hips

6. Resistance Band Rows

Sets: 2 | Reps: 8 | Rest: 60s

Target: back

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Chest up, shoulders down, control both pulling and releasing phases

7. Banded Squats

Sets: 2 | Reps: 8 | Rest: 60s

Target: quadriceps

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Maintain squat form, fight against band resistance

8. Drop Set: Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Sets: 2 | Reps: 8 | Rest: 60s

Target: shoulders

Intensity: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Key Points: Maintain strict overhead pressing form throughout all drops, no momentum or leg drive

🧘 Cooldown (5 minutes)

  • Deep breathing - 1 minute
  • Forward fold stretch - 30 seconds
  • Downward dog - 30 seconds
  • Child's pose - 45 seconds
  • Gentle spinal twist - 30 seconds each side
